               ABOUT US










DADA Architects, a Beijing-based firm founded in 2008 in Hong Kong, is a full-service design firm that has pioneered a holistic user-centered approach to architecture through a stunningly wide range of work: from master planning, urban design, interior architecture, space planning and even product design.


The Way We Work

As one of the most influential emerging young practices in contemporary China, DADA's work is re-defining Chinese design. The firm combines a deep understanding of Daoist and Confucius philosophy with a cutting-edge modern design aesthetic. The results are striking, modern designs that are inexplicably grounded in the history and culture of their location. Simultaneously, this is the efforts of DADA for Chinese architecture. DADA believes strongly in research as a design tool, as each project bears its unique set of contextual issues. A critical probing into the specificities of program, site, function, and history is essential to the creation of rigorous design work. Based on research, DADA desires to anchor its work on the dynamic interaction of experience, detail, material, form, and light rather than conforming to a formulaic style. The ultimate significance behind each project comes from how the built forms create meaning through their physical representations.


Achievements

In just the past few years, the firm has designed over 150 square kilometers of urban planning for Beijing, designed over Big Data Centre for a new city (Xiantao) near Chongqing (currently under construction), designed landmark bridges and buildings for new developments (such as the recently completed, Wenyu River Bridge No.2 and World Strawberry Congress Venue ), and designed over 29 premium theaters currently built or under construction. Believing that modern architecture most incorporate the latest advances in technology and art, DADA's partners are always looking for new sources of inspiration and collaboration to bring into their design. For example, DADA's successes outside of architecture have included the industrial design for a Silicon Valley healthcare product, Spire, that is sold in Apple Stores globally.


Awards & Publications

DADA’s work has won numerous awards, including National Design Awards(USA) , Future Science and Technology City International Design Awards, Beijing TBD core area International Design Awards, etc. and has been featured in publications such as Interior Design, Designboom, Archidaily, Skyscrapercity, Design-China, ID+C, Worldarchitecture, Contemporist, Architecturas, Arch2o, Arcilook, etc.
